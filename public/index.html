How to play

  1. Tap a tile next to the empty space to slide it into the gap.
  2. Keep sliding until the picture is restored — every piece in the right place.
  3. Your move count and timer start on your first move.
  4. Use Easy (3×3) or Hard (4×4) to change difficulty.
  5. Music mode uses a new album cover each day. After you solve it, scroll up for album details.
  6. You can play once per day. Check Info for your stats.

The reference thumbnail below the board shows the full image if you need a hint.
